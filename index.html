What on Mars is a sol?!

Sols work similar in the way days do on Earth, in the way that one sol is the length of one full rotation of Mars. However, since Mars is its own planet and has its own moons, its time must be calculated differently.

A single sol is approximately 24 hours, 39 minutes, and 35.24409 seconds.
